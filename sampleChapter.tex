\section{Sample Chapter}

This chapter will not be included in the final report, but includes a figure containing a sample EPS file. It also includes a table. Much more information on the AAS\TeX\ class is available at \texttt{http://ucpjournals.uchicago.edu/AAS/AASTeX/}. I'm willing to help in the preparation of EPS files.

%
% How to include a single eps figure
\begin{figure}
\epsscale{.25} % for twiddling the size of the figure
\plotone{cube.eps}
\caption{The cube}
\end{figure}

%
% How to include two side-by-side eps figures
\begin{figure}
\epsscale{.5} % for twiddling the size of the figure
\plottwo{cube.eps}{cube.eps}
\caption{Two more Platonic solids}
\end{figure}

%
% How to create a table
\begin{deluxetable}{lcr} % "lcr" = left, center, right align
\tablewidth{0pt} % sets table to natural width
% \rotate % puts table in landscape mode
\tablecaption{A Really Spiffing Title}
\tablehead{\colhead{Alpha} & \colhead{Beta} & \colhead{Gamma}}
\startdata
1 & 2 & 3\\
Able\tablenotemark{a} & Baker & Charlie\\
Groucho & Harpo & Chico\\
\enddata
\tablenotetext{a}{Now ``Alpha''}
\end{deluxetable}
